Privacy Policy

Application: AeroPrompt • Effective Date: January 14, 2026

At AeroPrompt, we consider the privacy of our users to be a fundamental priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and related services.

1. Data Collection & Usage by Tier

We believe in data minimization. We only collect what is necessary to provide you with our services. Our data handling practices differ based on your subscription tier:

Guest Users

Zero Persistence Policy: For users who are not logged in or are on the Guest tier, we do not store your chat history or input prompts on our servers. Your data is processed via API to generate a response and is immediately discarded by AeroPrompt after the session ends.

Free & Pro Users

Contextual Continuity: To enable advanced features like "Context Awareness" and "Personalization", we securely store a limited history (approximately the last 5-10 interactions) of your conversations. This data is used solely to provide continuity for your active sessions.

Control & Deletion: You retain full control over this data. You may clear your conversation history at any time directly through the extension's settings menu.

2. AI Models & Third-Party Processing

AeroPrompt utilizes the DeepSeek R1 reasoning model to power its prompt optimization and response generation.

  • Third-Party API Usage: Your prompts are transmitted via secure API to DeepSeek (Hangzhou DeepSeek Artificial Intelligence Co., Ltd.). This processing is necessary to provide the AI-generated content requested by the user.
  • Data Usage Limitations: Based on our API integration, we request that your inputs are processed for immediate output generation. However, users should be aware that third-party providers may have their own data retention policies as outlined in the DeepSeek Privacy Policy.
  • No Public Sharing: We do NOT share your data with public community feeds or standard public web interfaces. Your requests are sent specifically through private API channels.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We implement technical security measures designed to protect your information during transit and processing.

  • Transit Encryption: All data transmitted between the extension, our intermediary servers, and the DeepSeek API is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL (HTTPS) protocols.
  • API Security: Our API keys are stored in secure, encrypted environments and are never exposed to the client-side of the browser extension.
  • No Data Sale: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information or prompt history to third-party advertisers.

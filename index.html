Privacy Policy

Application: AeroPrompt • Last Updated: February 7, 2026

At AeroPrompt, we consider the privacy of our users to be a fundamental priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

When you sign in using Google, we collect the following information through the Chrome Identity API:

  • Email Address: Used to identify your account and manage your subscription
  • Display Name: Used for personalization within the extension
  • Profile Picture URL: Used to display your avatar in the extension

We only request access to basic profile information (openid, email, profile scopes). We do NOT have access to your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, or any other Google services.

Usage Data by Tier

Guest Users (Not Signed In)

Zero Persistence Policy: For users who are not logged in, we do not store your chat history or input prompts on our servers. Your data is transiently processed via encrypted API calls to generate a response and is immediately discarded by AeroPrompt.

Free & Pro Users (Signed In)

  • Contextual Continuity: We securely store a limited history (last 5-10 interactions) to enable context-aware prompt optimization
  • Subscription Status: We store your subscription tier and payment status
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage metrics to improve our service

Control & Deletion: You retain full control over this data. You may clear your conversation history at any time directly through the extension's settings menu.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate: Verify your identity and manage your account
  • Provide Services: Optimize prompts and deliver personalized features
  • Process Payments: Manage Pro subscriptions via Razorpay
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve Services: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance features

We do NOT use your data to:

  • Train AI models
  • Sell to third parties
  • Target advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

OpenRouter API

  • Your prompts are routed through OpenRouter for AI processing
  • We prioritize providers with Zero Data Retention (ZDR) policies
  • Your prompts are NOT used to train AI models

Google Authentication

  • We use Google Sign-In via the Chrome Identity API
  • Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Razorpay (Payment Processing)

  • Pro subscriptions are processed via Razorpay
  • We do NOT store your payment card details
  • Razorpay's privacy policy: https://razorpay.com/privacy/

4. Data Security & Encryption

We implement strict security measures to protect your information:

  • TLS/SSL Encryption: All communications are protected via industry-standard HTTPS
  • Secure Storage: User data is stored securely with encryption at rest
  • Metadata Anonymization: Usage analytics are anonymized and never linked to personal accounts
  • No Data Sale: We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information

5. Your Rights & Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the data we have collected about you
  • Delete: Clear your conversation history at any time through the extension settings
  • Account Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Opt-Out: Use the extension as a Guest without signing in

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Conversation History: Retained until you clear it or delete your account
  • Anonymous Analytics: Retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form

Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

AeroPrompt is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Chrome Extension Permissions

This extension uses the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
storage Save your preferences, settings, and history locally on your device
identity Enable Google Sign-In for secure account authentication and subscription management
host_permissions Inject the prompt optimizer on supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)

All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and are essential for the extension's core functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
